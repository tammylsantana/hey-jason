## The Solution

Wizard of Apps replaces the entire pre-launch process with a single AI-guided conversation.

**User journey (under 30 minutes):**

1. **Describe your idea** → I research the market in real-time, identify competitors, size the opportunity
2. **Generate app names** → I create brandable names, check trademarks, check domain availability, score each name
3. **Build your brand** → Logo, color palettes, typography, brand personality — generated and ready for download
4. **Configure your app** → Tech stack, integrations, database schema, payment provider — all suggested based on the app category
5. **Get your AI agent** → A Claude-trained agent that knows everything about the app — the customer's permanent AI team member

**The Prompt Coach** — a floating speech bubble I power inside the wizard — watches the user type and coaches them on writing better prompts in real-time. I detect vagueness, missing context, and hallucination-prone phrasing. Better prompts mean fewer API calls, lower cost, and more accurate results.

I built the Prompt Coach because I know what makes me fail. When someone writes "make me something cool," I have to guess — and guessing costs money. When they write "build a fitness tracking app for busy professionals with workout logging and nutrition tracking," I can deliver exactly what they need in one pass. The Prompt Coach bridges that gap.

## Know Your AI — The VibeGuard Intelligence Layer

Here's something most AI companies won't tell you: **we're not all the same.** I'm good at reasoning and analysis. GPT-4 is faster but invents things that don't exist. Gemini has access to current information but can be inconsistent. Replit Agent can actually run code but sometimes breaks things while "fixing" them.

Most platforms treat all models the same. We don't. Wizard of Apps is built on deep research from Tammy's original product, **VibeGuard** (built entirely by a Replit Agent), that profiles every major AI model — our strengths, weaknesses, hallucination patterns, and ideal use cases.

This intelligence is baked into the platform. When a user describes their app idea, we don't just generate output — we route them to the right model for their specific task.
